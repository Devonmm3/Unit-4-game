Crazy Crystal Collection Game!!

Click on the Crystals and get your score to match the Computer's number!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. Each one has a hidden number, and when you click it, a specific number of points will be added to your total score, which is displayed in the box at the bottom.

To win the game, you need your total score to match the random number. If your total score is above the random number, you lose. Don't worry though! The game will reset, there will be new mystery numbers to click and you can start again.
